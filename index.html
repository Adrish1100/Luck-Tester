LUCKY SPIN SLOT MACHINE

A Collaborative Project by

Adrish & Shreyansh

What We Built

A modern slot machine game inspired by casino machines. Players begin with 500 coins and spin three animated reels. Matching all three reels rewards coins while unsuccessful spins reduce the player's balance.

Features

  • Animated slot machine reels.
  • Random number generation.
  • Balance tracking system.
  • Win/Lose status messages.
  • Reset game anytime.
  • Modern casino-inspired dark theme.

Game Rules

  • Every player starts with 500 coins.
  • Each spin costs 50 coins.
  • If all three reels match, you win 100 coins.
  • If the reels do not match, 50 coins are deducted.
  • You cannot spin if your balance is 50 coins or less.
  • Press RESET to restore your balance and restart the game.
